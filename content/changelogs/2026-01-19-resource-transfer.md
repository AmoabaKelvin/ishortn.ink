Need to move your work to a different account? Resource transfer lets you migrate everything—links, custom domains, QR codes, folders, tags, and more—to another iShortn account in one smooth operation.

## What's new?

You can now transfer all your personal workspace resources to any other iShortn user. The transfer process is secure, requiring the recipient to explicitly accept the transfer before anything moves.

### What gets transferred

- **Links** — All your short links, with analytics history preserved
- **Custom domains** — Your branded domains move to the new account
- **QR codes** — Dynamic QR codes and their configurations
- **Folders** — Your organizational structure, merged intelligently
- **Tags** — Link tags, with smart name-based merging
- **UTM templates** — Your saved UTM parameter configurations
- **Analytics** — Complete click history stays with your links

### What stays behind

- **API tokens** — For security, tokens don't transfer
- **Subscription** — Your plan stays with your original account
- **Team memberships** — Team associations aren't affected

## How it works

1. **Initiate** — Go to Settings and enter the recipient's email address
2. **Validate** — We check that the recipient exists and has room for your resources
3. **Send request** — The recipient gets an email with a secure link
4. **Accept** — They review and accept (or decline) the transfer
5. **Done** — Resources move instantly, and both parties get notified

The entire process is designed to be safe and reversible until the final acceptance.

## Smart merging

If the recipient already has folders or tags with the same names as yours, we merge them intelligently:

- **Folders** — Links move into the existing folder with the same name
- **Tags** — Links get associated with the existing tag

No duplicates, no conflicts. Everything ends up organized.

## Plan limits respected

Before a transfer can proceed, we verify the recipient's account can accommodate all resources. If accepting the transfer would exceed their plan limits (links, domains, folders), the transfer is blocked with a clear explanation of what's over the limit.

## Security features

- **7-day expiry** — Transfer requests expire if not acted upon
- **Email verification** — Only the intended recipient can accept
- **Atomic operation** — The entire transfer succeeds or fails together
- **Decline option** — Recipients can explicitly decline, notifying the sender

## Who can use this?

Resource transfer is available to all users. Find it in [Settings](/dashboard/settings) under the Transfer section. You can only transfer resources from your personal workspace—team resources stay with their teams.
