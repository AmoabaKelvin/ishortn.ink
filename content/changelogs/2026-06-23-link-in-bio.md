iShortn now does more than one link at a time. Build a single public bio page — the kind you put in an Instagram, TikTok, or X profile — that holds all your important links, social profiles, and a short intro. And because it's built on iShortn, every link on the page is a real tracked short link.

## What's new

A new **Bio Pages** section in your dashboard lets you create a page at `ishortn.ink/p/yourname`. It comes with a fast, mobile-first builder and a live preview beside it.

### Blocks

Add and reorder blocks with drag and drop:

- **Link buttons** — each one becomes a tracked short link automatically
- **Social icons** — X, Instagram, TikTok, GitHub, and more
- **Headings and text** — to introduce yourself or group your links
- **Email button** and **dividers**

### Themes

Pick from a set of curated themes, or customize the accent color, button style, background, and font to match your brand.

### Tracking built in

This is the part other link-in-bio tools charge for. Every link block routes through your existing short-link pipeline, so clicks land in your analytics — country, device, and referrer included. The page has its own analytics too: views, unique visitors, click-through rate, and a per-link breakdown.

### QR code

Every bio page has a QR code that points straight at it — ready for print, slides, or a table tent.

## Plans

- **Free** — 1 bio page with the iShortn badge
- **Pro** — 3 pages, custom domains, full theme customization, branding removal, and a custom social-preview image
- **Ultra** — unlimited pages, scheduled blocks, per-block UTM attribution, and team-managed pages

## Get started

Head to [Bio Pages](/dashboard/bio-pages) and pick a handle. Build it as a draft and publish when you're ready.
