Never miss a moment. You can now set click milestones on any link and receive an email notification the instant your link crosses a threshold you care about.

## What's new?

### Milestone notifications

Set specific click targets on your links — like 100, 1,000, or 10,000 clicks — and we'll send you an email as soon as your link reaches each one. It's a simple way to stay on top of how your links are performing without constantly checking your dashboard.

### Easy setup

When you edit a link, you'll find a new "Click Milestones" section. Setting up milestones is quick:

- **Preset thresholds** — Pick from common milestones like 100, 250, 500, 1,000, 5,000, or 10,000 clicks
- **Custom thresholds** — Enter any number and press Enter to add your own target
- **Remove anytime** — Click the X on any milestone to remove it

Milestones that have already been reached are highlighted in green so you can see your progress at a glance.

### How it works

1. **Edit a link** — Open any link from your dashboard
2. **Expand Click Milestones** — Click the section to reveal the milestone editor
3. **Add thresholds** — Pick from presets or type a custom number
4. **Save** — Click "Save Changes" and you're done

Once a milestone is reached, you'll get an email with your link name, the milestone crossed, and the current total click count. Each milestone only fires once — no duplicate notifications.

## Plan limits

Click milestone notifications are available on paid plans:

- **Pro** — Up to 5 milestones per link
- **Ultra** — Unlimited milestones per link

## Who can use this?

Click milestones are available to **Pro** and **Ultra** subscribers. Set your first milestone by editing any link from your [dashboard](/dashboard).
