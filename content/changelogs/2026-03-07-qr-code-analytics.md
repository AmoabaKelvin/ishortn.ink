QR codes now have their own analytics. Previously, you had to create a short link first and then select it when creating a QR code in order to track scans. Now, every QR code tracks scans automatically — no extra setup needed.

## What's new?

### Analytics built into every QR code

Just enter your destination URL when creating a QR code and scan tracking is set up for you. No need to create a short link beforehand or configure anything else.

### Dedicated analytics page

Click any QR code card to view its full analytics. You get the same detailed insights available for short links:

- **Total and unique scans** — See how many times your QR code has been scanned and by how many distinct visitors
- **Top country and referrer** — Identify where your scans are coming from at a glance
- **Scans over time** — A bar chart showing scan activity across your selected date range
- **Geographic breakdown** — Country, city, and continent-level scan distribution
- **Device and browser stats** — Know what devices and browsers your audience uses
- **Referrer breakdown** — See which apps and surfaces are driving scans
- **World map heatmap** — Visualize global scan distribution on an interactive map (Pro)

### Simpler creation flow

The creation page has been streamlined. Instead of having to create a short link first and then come back to select it, you now just enter a destination URL and you are done.

## Who can use this?

QR code analytics are available to all users. Pro users get access to extended date ranges and the world map heatmap. View your QR codes at [/dashboard/qrcodes](/dashboard/qrcodes).
